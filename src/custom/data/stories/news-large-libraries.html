Tips for Managing Large Icon Libraries

As your icon collection grows, keeping it organized becomes increasingly important. Here are some tips for managing large icon libraries with Icon Mason.

1. Use Consistent Naming

Establish a naming convention for your icons. Use descriptive, kebab-case names like arrow-left or user-profile. This makes searching much easier.

2. Leverage Tags

Icon Mason automatically extracts tags from artboard names when exporting. Use this to your advantage by naming artboards with relevant keywords separated by dashes.

3. Organize by IconSet

Group related icons into IconSets within your IconJar. For example:

  • Navigation icons
  • Social media icons
  • Action icons
  • Object icons

4. Use Search Effectively

Icon Mason's fuzzy search is powerful. You don't need exact matches – searching for "arrow" will find "arrows", "arrow-left", and similar terms.

5. Regular Maintenance

Periodically review your icon library and remove duplicates or outdated icons. This keeps your collection lean and efficient.
